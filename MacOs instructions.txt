- Copy askAi.app into your Application directory
- Launch askAi.shortcut, a window pops up, click on "Add This shortcut"

Usage:

- Launch LM studio
- Copy some text in your favorite App
- press control + option + command
- askAi Launches and starts generating based on the clipboard content

Notes:
- if the askAi is already launched, you don't need to use the shortcut, just activate the app it will look for changes automagically
- In case of error, a message is displayed in the statusbar.
